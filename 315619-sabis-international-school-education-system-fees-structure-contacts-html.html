Sabis International School education system, fees structure, contacts

In September 2018, Sabis International School welcomed its first batch of students and revealed plans of expansion almost immediately. Sabis is a state-of-the-art learning institution with some of the best facilities and the latest technology aimed at improving the education process. The latter includes E-books and interactive whiteboards, to mention a few.

Through the Sabis education system, students gain practical skills and become well-rounded individuals. The school accepts candidates from different nationalities, backgrounds, and races.

A brief history; who owns Sabis International School?

Sabis School was opened in February 2018 and welcomed its first students in September the same year. The school is owned by Centum Investments in collaboration with Sabis Education Network. It sits on 20 acres of land in a leafy suburb, and an aerial view of the school and its location can justify the expansion claims.

Sabis also has some of the best contemporary sports facilities ranging from a multi-door gymnasium, multiple basketballs and tennis courts and a state-of-the-art 200-meter running track. It also has a 400-meter running track with a field and a Semi-Olympic heated swimming pool.

Very few schools in Kenya can afford a 400-seater performing arts theatre for their performing arts and musical performances part of the curriculum. The institution is considered as a dynamic community with administrators, teachers, and students from different parts of the globe. In case you are wondering about the Sabis International School address, the institution is located in the popular Runda Estate off Kiambu Road.

Unity in diversity

According to the school's website, everyone associated with the institution always has a role to play. However, the expected results are learned students who have a deeper understanding of the world and the different cultures therein. Accountability is held in high regard at the institution with administrators, students, and staff required to undergo self-audit to expose and correct any errors.

An overview of the Sabis International School curriculum

This school in Runda might be just over a year old, but its curriculum is recognized globally. They offer a wide range of external examinations ranging from British IGCSEs, US Advanced Placement Exams, and A-Levels. While all the courses offered are age-appropriate, students at Sabis School are encouraged to hold themselves in high standards. They aid them in doing this through workbooks and textbooks prepared by the Sabis Academic Development Division.

Currently considered one of the best international schools in Kenya, Sabis has a flexible curriculum seeing it allows for the accommodation of students at different levels throughout an academic year. The institution focuses primarily on the English and Mathematics subjects and uses the former as the official means of instruction/communication.

Mathematics is considered a prerequisite for understanding natural sciences and any other related subjects with ease. Despite the primary focus on English as the first language, the school stresses the need for a second language throughout the curriculum.

Students take up learning Swahili as the second language and French as the third from KG1. All courses have a clear structure for precise teaching with tutors explaining a single concept until it is understood using the Sabis Point System. Lessons are taught using tactics like individual work, group work, and oral tests to guarantee total concentration. Students are also engaged in exams, which have not been seen by the teachers to ensure accountability.
