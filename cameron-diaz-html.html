Actress who debuted in the 1994 comedy The Mask. She received Golden Globe Award nominations for her roles in There's Something About Mary, Being John Malkovich, Vanilla Sky and Gangs of New York. She also played leading roles in Charlie's Angels, Bad Teacher and The Other Woman. 

Before Fame

She modeled for Levi's and Calvin Klein between 1988 and 1993.  

Trivia

She voiced the character Princess Fiona in the Shrek film series from 2001 to 2010. 

Family Life

In January 2015, she got married to Good Charlotte's Benji Madden. She and Madden welcomed a daughter named Raddix in December 2019. In March 2024, they welcomed a son, Cardinal Madden together.

Associated With

She co-starred with Kate Winslet, Jude Law and Jack Black in the 2006 romantic comedy The Holiday.
