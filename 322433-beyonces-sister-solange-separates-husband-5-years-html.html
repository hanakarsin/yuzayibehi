Beyonce's sister Solange separates from husband after 5 years

- Solange Knowles has revealed that her and husband Alan Ferguson are no longer together

- The couple tied the knot in 2014 in New Orleans in a wedding ceremony attended by Beyonce and Jay-z

- They did not have a child together, though she has a 15-year-old son with former husband, Daniel Smith

Beyonce's sister Solange Knowles has called it quits in her marriage of close to five years with her husband Alan Ferguson .

Solange aged 33-years-old has been together with Allan for more than 11 years and tied the knot in 2014.

However, Knowles revealed that they parted ways earlier in the year with their 5th wedding anniversary approaching.

In a Instagram post, the singer opened up about her life saying the past two years brought her more physical and spiritual transition and evolution.

"The past 2 years have brought me more physical and spiritual transition and evolution than ever before, my body left me with no choice but to listen and be still. Within that stillness I begin my journey in confronting my worst enemy, fear," wrote Solange.

The mother of one explained living her best and worst moment in front of the lens since she was a teeneger.

"I have lived my best and worst moments in front of the lens and gaze of the world since I was a teenager. I have always tried to live in my truth no matter how ugly or full of love it is. I have also tried to carve out the space to protect my heart, and my life as it unfolds, evolves, and changes," she added.

Solange reminiscenced her moments back from the day she met her estranged husband and praised him.

"11 years ago I met a phenomenal man who changed every existence of my life. Early this year we separated and parted ways, I find it necessary to protect the sacredness of my personal truth and to live in it fully just as I have before and will continue to do," she wrote.

In her post, she insisted that she was not going to reveal the reasons for the split as it is nobody's business.

"It is unfair to not have power of your own story as you shape and mold and rewrite it yourself. A nigga ain’t perfect, but I am leaning into the fear of the unknown and all the glory and power I know exist within God and the universes grace. May all of your transitions no matter how big or small, be kind to you and filled with incredible love and light!" she concluded.

Solange married Alan in New Orleans in November 2014 and their lavish wedding was attended by her superstar sister Beyonce and brother-in-law Jay-Z.

She has a 15-year-old son, Daniel Julez, with her first husband, Daniel Smith and did not have any children with Alan.

Ferguson is a director, and he's done several big-time music videos among them Solange's acclaimed Cranes in the Sky and also, Don't Touch My Hair.
