Loise Kim Thanks Fans for Praying for Dad While He Was in ICU: &quot;He Wasn't Talking&quot;

May 2024 Â· 2 minute read
  • Kikuyu gospel singer Loise Kim thanked her social media fans for standing by her when her dad was in an Intensive Care Unit (ICU)
  • Kim divulged that her father who she really adores, was in pathetic health condition as he couldn't talk or eat
  • She told the fans that her dad was recently discharged and he is now well

Kikuyu gospel singer Loise Kim took to social media to give gratitude to her social media fans for praying for her dad while he was admitted to an Intensive Care Unit (ICU).

On a new note to Facebook, the singer felt proud and thankful to the fans for standing by her.

Kim told them that she was strengthened by their warm messages in her comment section when she posted about the sickness of her father.

She revealed that her father was discharged from the hospital a few days ago and she expressed faith that her dad will be fully back to his feet.

He had a flurry of complications as noted by Kim.

"I can't express how appreciative I am for all your comments, love, prayers and financial support that you gave to us when my dad was sick. Thank you for being there for us when we needed you the most. He was discharged from the hospital a while ago," Kim said.

According to Kim, her dad's condition was pathetic when he was booked for ICU as the old man couldn't talk, eat normally and was only breathing through his neck.

He was eating directly through the stomach, he couldn't talk either walk and he was breathing through the neck. Fortunately today, I bring to you this man whom God has healed. We lost hope but God proved himself to us," she added.
