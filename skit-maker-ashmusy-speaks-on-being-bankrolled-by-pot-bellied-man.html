Skit Maker, Ashmusy Speaks On Being Bankrolled By 'Pot-Bellied' Man

February 2024 · 1 minute read

Nigerian skit maker, Amarachi Amusi, popular known as Ashmusy, has reacted to the speculations that she is being sponsored by a ‘pot-bellied’ man spotted in her recent social media post.

Naija News reports that this is following her latest acquisition of car and vacation photos and videos which emerged online.

Some Nigerians claimed that the ‘pot-bellied’ man spotted in the videos was her sugar daddy and he is responsible for her luxurious lifestyle.

Reacting to this, Ashmusy in a post shared on her Instagram page rained curses on those attributing her success and achievement to a man.

She said those belittling her hard work and spreading fake news about her will also experience fruitless labour.

She wrote: “Anybody that ever belittles my hustle and attaches my success/achievement to a man will never ever eat the fruit of their labour. He/she will work and not make anything from the work. If you ever decide to give my glory to any man and speak fake news against me…

“If you ever make all my shooting of videos every damn day look like nothing, if you make my running business back to back for 5 years now look like nothing… you will work so hard and not see results.”
