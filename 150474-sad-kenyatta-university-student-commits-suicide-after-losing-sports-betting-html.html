Kenyatta University student commits suicide after losing bet

- Kenyatta University student has hanged himself after losing KSh 80,000 in sports betting

- The student had placed half the money, meant for his college fees, on Germany and the other half on Italy win in normal time during their Euro quarter final match; the teams drew 1-1 in normal time

- He hanged himself behind his mother's house in Uriri, Migori county, after the loss

A university student from Migori county has hanged himself after losing thousands of shillings in sports betting.

The Kenyatta University student committed suicide on Wednesday, July 6, after losing KSh 80,000 during the Germany vs Italy Euro quarter final match.

The deceased, who was staying in Nairobi, travelled to his village in Uriri after the loss and hanged himself behind his mother's house.

He left behind a note indicating that the money he was given to pay college fees had disappeared in betting and he saw no need of living, the paper reported.

The Saturday, July 2, match went into extra time after the teams drew 1-1. Germany later beat Italy 6-5 in the penalties.

A similar incident happened in Bungoma. A man hanged himself after losing KSh 47,000 on sports betting.

In April, a student from Maseno University had to drop out of school when he bet and lost KSh 40,000 on a football match.

Another man working in Nairobi as a bank manager was also left by his wife after he bet and lost KSh 500,000 which was said to be a loan.
