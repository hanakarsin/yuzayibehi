PDP To Hold Strategic Stakeholders Meeting

August 2024 · 2 minute read

The South-south Zonal Working Committee of the Peoples Democratic Party (PDP) is set to hold expanded zonal stakeholders meeting on the 14th March 2022 ahead of the 2023 election.

The decision to hold the meeting was taken at the South-south zonal meeting held at its headquarters in Port Harcourt, Rivers State, on Tuesday.

In a statement signed by PDP zonal spokesperson, Bishop Etim Ante, and sent to Naija News, the party disclosed that the meeting will be held in Akwa Ibom on the fourth day of March.

The PDP hailed governors, legislators and relevant stakeholders of the party for their unflinching support, urging them to continue with their good work.

The statement reads: “Arising from a Zonal Working Committee meeting of the Peoples Democratic Party (PDP) South South, held at the zonal headquarters, Port Harcourt, Rivers State, on the 14th March 2022.

“The Party took the following resolutions;

“(1) The meeting resolved to organize a SPECIAL STAKEHOLDERS MEETING in Akwa Ibom State on the 4th day of March,2022 to fortify and strengthen the Party in the zone.

“(2) The Meeting shall be attended by strategic stakeholders of the Party at various levels. This includes the hierarchy of the party structure, Former and serving top officers of the Party, Executive and legislative arms of government , both at the National and State levels of PDP family and other critical party leaders across the zone.

“The zone appreciates our Governors, Legislators and relevant stakeholders of the party for their unflinching support and urge them to continue with their good work which has brought our dear Party to the limelight and made PDP the pride of the Nation.

“The zone resolved to continue to work in harmony with all the party structures, the Government and Legislatures of our party to bring the party in the zone and beyond, to a greater height.”
