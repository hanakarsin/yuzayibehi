Kemi Olunloyo Reacts As Tonto Dikeh Storms Lagos To Send Her Back To Jail

August 2024 · 2 minute read

Controversial Nigerian journalist Kemi Olunloyo has reacted to Tonto Dikeh’s threat to get her arrested.

Recall that Naija News earlier reported that Tonto revealed via an Instagram post that she had arrived in Lagos to arrest the self-acclaimed investigative journalist.

Tonto and Kemi have been having a back and forth for some time now. This was due to an Instagram live the journalist held with the Nollywood actress’s ex-boyfriend Prince Kpokpogri over his demolished building.

In the video, Olunloyo called Tonto a clout chaser and a drug user.

Responding to Olunloyo, Tonto took to her social media story to shade her with threats of sending her to jail.

Feeling hurt by the actress’ comment, Olunloyo asked why Tonto is wishing she goes back to jail after all she went through in Port Harcourt prison, which has made her battle with Post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD) for years.

Tonto Dikeh promised not to back down as she stated that she would help curb Kemi’s vile mouth and thereafter took a flight to Lagos.

Reacting to the actress’s arrival, Olunloyo took to her Instagram page to question the reason Tonto is obsessed with her.

“Ms Tonto Dikeh The obsession she has for me is typical of an active drug user. I have invited her to join in the war on drugs via education instead of begging the @ndlea_nigeria to test her and make her an ambassador. Tonto start building yourself and stop erratic behaviour”.
